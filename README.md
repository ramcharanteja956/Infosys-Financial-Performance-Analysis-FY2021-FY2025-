**Infosys — Financial Performance Analysis (FY2020–FY2025)**

A comprehensive, end-to-end financial analysis of Infosys Limited, one of India's largest IT services companies, built entirely from publicly available Annual Reports. The project spans six fiscal years (FY2020–FY2025) and delivers a structured Excel financial model alongside a multi-page interactive Power BI dashboard.

***Project Structure***

infosys-financial Performance Analysis (FY2020-FY2025)/
│
├── "Infosys Financial Statements.xlsx"

Power BI interactive dashboard
└── README.md

***What's Inside***

**Excel Model — 4 Sheets**
- sheet 1.Income Statement :
 Revenue → Gross Profit → EBITDA → EBIT → PBT → PAT, EPS, and key margins
- sheet 2.Balance Sheet :
 Full asset, equity & liability structure with a built-in balance check
- sheet 3.Cash Flow Statement : 
 CFO, CFI, CFF, Cash at the End of the Year; FCF and earnings quality metrics
- sheet 4.Ratio Analysis :
 30+ ratios across 7 categories
    
**Power BI Dashboard — 6 Pages**
- **Executive Summary**:
 KPI cards, Revenue & Profit Growth Journey, FCF Performance, Shareholder Return trends
- **Income Statement Analysis**:
   Revenue Growth Trajectory, Profitability Waterfall (Revenue → PAT), Growth Momentum chart
- **Balance Sheet Analysis**:
 Asset Composition Trend, Capital Structure Evolution, Liquidity Strength, Working Capital Position
- **CashFlow Statement Analysis**:
 Cash Flow Drivers by Activity (CFO/CFI/CFF), Earnings Quality Ratio, Shareholder Distribution Trends, FCF Margin Trend
- **Ratio Analysis**:
 Interactive table with 7 ratio categories (Growth, Profitability, Liquidity, Efficiency, Solvency, Cash Flow, DuPont) with Trend & Status flags
- **Insights**:
 Top 5 Strengths & Risks, Management Focus Areas, CFO View, CEO View, FY2025 vs FY2021 Strategic Performance Index

***Tools Used***

**Microsoft Excel** — Financial modelling, ratio computation, trend analysis
**Power BI** — Interactive dashboard and data visualisation
**Data Source** — Infosys Annual Reports & Consolidated Financial Statements (publicly available)

***Purpose***

This project was built to practise and demonstrate:
- Reading and structuring real-world annual report data
- Building clean, formula-driven financial models from scratch
- Computing and interpreting financial ratios across multiple years
- Translating raw numbers into actionable business insights
- Designing professional, multi-page dashboards in Power BI
